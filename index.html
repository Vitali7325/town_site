Irpen (Ukrainian: Ірпі́нь) is a city in the Bucha district of the Kyiv region of Ukraine, the administrative center of the Irpen urban community.

Irpin (Ukrainian: Ірпінь, IPA: [irˈp⁽ʲ⁾inʲ]) is a city on the Irpin River in Bucha Raion, Kyiv Oblast, northern Ukraine. It is located next to the capital Kyiv. Irpin hosts the administration of Irpin urban hromada, one of the hromadas of Ukraine. The city has a population of 65,167 (2022 estimate).The city has a railway station built in 1899. In 2022, it received the title Hero City of Ukraine.

In the 2022 Battle of Irpin during the Russian invasion of Ukraine, 70% of the city was damaged. As of June 25, 2023, the city's mayor reported that most people had already returned: "Yes, we have already returned 85% of the entire city's population. In addition, we have received almost 25,000 internally displaced persons, mostly from the east.
